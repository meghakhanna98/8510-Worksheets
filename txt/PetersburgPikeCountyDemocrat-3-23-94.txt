Petersburg Pike County Democrat March 23, 1894 pg. 2
Dr. Frank Powell (White Beaver) returned to La Crosse, Wis., on the 16th, from the home of Col. Cody (Buffalo Bill), in North Platte, Neb., and reported that all possibility of a duel between Cody and Fred May was past, May and Cody having adjusted their differences through their representatives.
