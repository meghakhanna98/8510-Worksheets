Logansport Journal December 12, 1883 pg..
Four boys, son of wealthy residents of the Fourth Ward of Milwaukee, have been arrested for setting the recent incendiary fires. Forming a "Buffalo Bill organization," they built a "pirate's den," in which they kept dime novels, pistols and lassos, and occasionally raided the pantries of wealthy people, whose mysterious loss of jellies and pies led to the lads' arrests.
