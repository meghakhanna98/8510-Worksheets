Goshen Democrat July 1, 1910 pg. 2

PROMOTION

SETTLE WARFARE

The Hagenbeck-Wallace and John Robinson circuses have settled their Indiana time warfare. The Hagenbeck-Wallace shows take Fort Wayne, Columbia City and Plymouth, while Robinson gets Warsaw and Valparaiso. 

Buffalo Bill-Pawnee Bill’s long time ahead advertising has kept other shows out of Goshen. The two bills are due here July 27th.
