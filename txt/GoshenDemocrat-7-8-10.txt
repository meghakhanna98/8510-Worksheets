Goshen Democrat July 8, 1910 pg. 1

PROMOTION

NO STREET PARADE

The Buffalo Bill-Pawnee Bill Wild West and Far East shows, due here Thursday, July 27, will not give a street parade. Free exhibitions will be confined to seeing the horses, elephants and camels on the Wurster lot on East Purl street, where the arena will be placed. 

Buffalo Bill (Col. Cody) is about 65 years of age and this is his farewell season in the saddle. He was last here 14 years ago, Aug. 13, 1896.
