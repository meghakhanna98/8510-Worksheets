Terre Haute Weekly Gazette August 12, 1886 pg. 5
Buffalo Bill's Prospective Wealth. NEW YORK, Aug. 11.-Wm. F. Cody, better known as "Buffalo Bill," received word today from his lawyer at Cleveland, that he is likely to win his suit for the property on Euclid Avenue, Cleveland. The land is valued at $1,500,000, and was illegally transferred by a grand uncle of Mr. Cody.
