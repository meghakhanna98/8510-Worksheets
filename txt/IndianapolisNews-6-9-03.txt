Indianapolis News June 9 1903
EVANSVILLE-The State convention of the Supreme Herd of Buffaloes in this city on Thursday will be minus the attendance of William F. Cody (Buffalo Bill), Most Noble Honorable Ranchman, who is now in Europe, but other distinguished members of the order are expected. Chris W. Kratz will be chief marshal; George P. Heilman will have charge of the addresses, and fire chief Dunlevy will do the herding.
