Daily Tribune Dec. 30 1902
Marries Buffalo Bill's Daughter. 
CHEYENNE. Wyo., Dec. 30-Lieutenant Clarence Smott, troop G, Tenth cavalry, stationed at Fort Mackenzie, has gone to New York, where he will be married to Miss Irma Cody, youngest daughter of Colonel W. F. Cody. He met the young woman while on a practice march with his troop.
