Hagerstown Wayne Farmer May 22 1908
Col. William F. Cody
WASHINGTON. May 19.-Col. William F. Cody's (Buffalo Bill) twenty fifth anniversary as an exhibitor of typical American wild life was celebrated today in Washington. Government officials, army officers and many long-time friends of Col. Cody participated. The anniversary performances of Buffalo
Bill's show last night and today were attended by members of the President's family. Gen. Eugene A. Carr, Wesley Merritt and twenty other general officers of the army with whom Col. Cody served in the early days of the Western plains. Quentin Roosevelt rode in the Deadwood coach and made the acquaintances of the noted Indian chiefs with the show.
