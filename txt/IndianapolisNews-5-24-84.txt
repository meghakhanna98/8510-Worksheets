Indianapolis News May 24, 1884 pg. 4
The route of the parade of the Buffalo Bill troupe on Monday, at 10 o'clock, will be from the Exposition on Alabama street to Washington, thence west to Mississippi, thence south to Maryland, thence east to Illinois, south on Illinois to Louisiana, east to Meridian, north on Meridian around the Circle to Pennsylvania, north on Pennsylvania to Massachusetts avenue, on the avenue to Delaware, and return to the grounds. This troupe will give exhibitions at the Exposition grounds on Monday and Tuesday afternoons. It has no connection with the Carver Wild West troupe, which is stranded at Terre Haute.
