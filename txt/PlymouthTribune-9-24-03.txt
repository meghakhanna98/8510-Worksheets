Plymouth Tribune Sept. 24 1903
Chief Hadji Cheriff, the celebrated Arabian chief, who has traveled with Barnum & Bailey, Buffalo Bill and many other entertainments in all parts of the world, called at our office Tuesday. He is with the King of the Desert Company. He speaks sixteen languages, is 72 years old, but does not look more than 55.  He says he will run a foot race with any man in Plymouth.
