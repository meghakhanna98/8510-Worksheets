New Albany Ledger January 30, 1883 pg. 4
Andy Zoeller, arrested yesterday by Marshal Fein and Officer Richards, for stealing, was arraigned before Mayor Kent this forenoon and entered a plea of guilty to stealing a saw and axe from J., M. & L. railroad, and was held in $150 to answer the circuit court. His theft of two rifles, valued at $100, from Buffalo Bill, will go before the grand jury.
