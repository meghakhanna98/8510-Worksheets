Monroeville Weekly Breeze Oct. 31 1901 pg 7

Buffalo Bill Wrecked
A Southern Railroad train, bearing Buffalo Bill's Wild West show, was wrecked at Linwood, N. C. Four show cars were smashed and a hundred horses were killed. The trainmaster, engineer and fireman of the show train were hurt. The accident was caused by the engineer of train No. 75 mistaking his orders and running on the time of the show train.
