Ft. Wayne Evening Post June 22 1896 pg 1
Mr. John M. Burke generally known as the "Major" in advance with Buffalo Bill's great Wild West show which appears in Fort Wayne next Monday is in the city. The major says that he wants the public to understand that Mr. Cody, (Buffalo Bill,) appears at each and every performance and that the show is given on the same gigantic scale as it was in Chicago during the great World's fair, and Chicago one week ago.
