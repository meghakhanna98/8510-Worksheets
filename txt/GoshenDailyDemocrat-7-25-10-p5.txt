Goshen Daily Democrat July 25, 1910 pg. 1

PROMOTION

Cost of Transportation

The Big Four will tomorrow night haul the Buffalo Bill-Pawnee Bill show trains, in all 50 cars, from Benton Harbor to Goshen. It costs the show $297 for the jump. All the paraphernalia will be unloaded at Purl and Ninth streets and the arena will be placed on the Wurster lot on East Purl street. The Lake Shore will handle the trains from Goshen to South Bend.
