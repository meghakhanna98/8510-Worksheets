Crawfordsville Journal Wednesday July 28, 1909 page 3

Buffalo Bill’s Wild West Show, with its gorgeous pageant, its funny clowns, its pretty women and fine horses, may come, and give us a day’s pleasure; we’re all glad to see it come – and go. But we’ve got a show going all this week; doesn’t come for to-day and gone to-morrow; that will be more interesting to those who were too busy during the past two weeks to attend our big 

Clearing Sale

We will continue the sale for 6 days, until Saturday, July 31st.
