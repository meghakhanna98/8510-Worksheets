Indianapolis Evening Minute October 16, 1885 pg. 2
A Juvenile "Buffalo Bill" Killed 
New Brighton, Staten Island, Oct. 16.- Lemuel Collins, alias "Buffalo Bill," fifteen years old, was met this morning by Wm. Sullivan, aged sixteen, armed with a gun. Collins refused to throw up his hands when Sullivan commanded, and the latter fired twelve buckshot in his head and body, mortally wounding him. Sullivan was arrested.
