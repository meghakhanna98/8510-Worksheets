Indianapolis Journal October 4 1899
The President at Terre Haute. Special to the Indianapolis Journal. TERRE HAUTE, Ind., Oct. 3. Congressman Farls has telegraphed from Washington that President McKinley has consented to stop in Terre Haute In the afternoon next Wednesday on his way from the Blue and Gray reunion, in Evansville, back to Chicago. It is the week of the street fair and the same day that Buffalo Bill will be here. President McKinley visited the Terre Haute street fair a year ago.
