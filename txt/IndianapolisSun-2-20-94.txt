Indianapolis Sun February 20, 1894 pg. 2
Buffalo Bill has political aspirations- wants to be governor of his state. The other day he met that swell head of swelldom, Fred May, notorious as a New York club man. May was anxious to pick a quarrel with the genial Cody, and the latter promptly knocked him down. If Bill will keep on at the good work and wipe a few such blooded brawlers as May off the earth, he may have any place of political preferment he yearns for.
