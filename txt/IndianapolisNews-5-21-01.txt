Indianapolis News, May 21 1901 pg. 3
Mr. and Mrs. Joseph Miller will give a Buffalo Bill party this evening, their guests being Mrs. Major P. Dale, Mr. and Mrs. Shoergens and daughter. Miss Alma. Miss Muriel Hitt gave a small company to-day for Miss Helen Birch, of Chicago, first having a luncheon at the Columbia Club, and later going to the Buffalo Bill matinee.
