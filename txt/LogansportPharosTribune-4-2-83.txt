Logansport Pharos Tribune April 2, 1883 pg. 4
W. F. Cody (Buffalo Bill) and Dr. W. F. Carver, the rifle shot, are reported as about to organize at North Platte, Neb., an outdoor combination, and have engaged 200 Indians, together with [80] Mexican vaqueros, cowboys, etc., who are to illustrate life in the Western wilds.
