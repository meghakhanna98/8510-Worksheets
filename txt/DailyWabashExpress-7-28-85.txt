Daily Wabash Express July 28, 1885 pg. 4
Another Salisbury Gets Distinction. Boston, Mass., July 27.-Mr. Nate Salisbury, half owner of Buffalo Bill's wild west show, was to-day made chief of the Un-Ka-Pa-Pa tribe, of which Sitting Bull is chief. The ceremonies were conducted by Sitting Bull and were very impressive and solemn. Mr. Salisbury is the first white man ever made chief of this tribe. He was given the name of Wali-Sech-Sha-E -Ton-Sha, which is the Indian for "Little White Chief."
