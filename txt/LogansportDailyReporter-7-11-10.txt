Logansport Daily Reporter July 11, 1910 pg. 8

PROMOTION

THE CITY IN BRIEF

(Part of a larger article with various bits of news from around town)

W. J. Willis, advance man for the Buffalo Bill shows, who was in the city Friday, was mistaken for Bob Fitzsimmons by a traveling man here. Willis has the appearance of the ex-pugilist, and was taken for the fighter two years ago at Lafayette.
