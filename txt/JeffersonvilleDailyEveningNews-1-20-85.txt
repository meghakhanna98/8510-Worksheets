Jeffersonville Daily Evening News January 20, 1885 pg. 1
Cincinnati, Jan. 20- W.F Cody (Buffalo Bill), Capt. A. H. Bogardus and Nat Salsbury, doing business as the Buffalo Bill Wild West Show, have entered a suit for $20,000 against owners of the steamer W. P. Thompson. The plaintiffs claim $12,000 for the loss of their stock, etc., and $8.00 for the loss of business by the sinking of the steamer which had been represented as seaworthy.
