Fort Wayne Sentinel August 11, 1886 pg. 1
Buffalo Bill's Prospective Wealth 
New York, Aug. 11- Wm. F. Cody, better known as "Buffalo Bill", received word to-day from his lawyer at Cleveland that he is likely to win his suit for the property on Euclid avenue, Cleveland. The land is valued at $1,500,000, and was illegally transferred by a grand uncle of Mr. Cody.
