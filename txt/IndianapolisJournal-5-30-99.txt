The Indianapolis journal May 30, 1899
W. H. Holland Arrested.
NEW YORK, May 29. Wm. H. Holland, of Albany. N. Y., the bookmaker, who, on April 2d last, shot Samuel Waller, the ticket seller for Buffalo Bill's Wild West show in a saloon in this city, and who escaped after the shooting, was arrested here today and held in $500 bail for examination on a charge of felonious assault.
