Indianapolis Star July 11, 1911 pg. 6

EXTRANEOUS

***Untitled***

If W. C. Brown, the railroad executive, and Buffalo Bill both break into the United States Senate, won’t there be a calamitous wall set up? They will say that the man whose genius has successfully operated a great arterial road, giving thousands of men employment, is of the “interests,” and this Cody person will be lambasted as worse than a circus clown.
