Indianapolis Sun Aug. 28 1897

Buffalo Bill's Indians do a very creditable hold-up in the show when the old stage coach, rolls over the arena. But how much more completely and tastily could they do the job if they would but stop in Indianapolis for a few days and snatch the "local financiers," aided by those two bandits, the News and Sentinel, and the mighty nail-eating Amazon, the journal, attempting to encompass and scalp street car company.
