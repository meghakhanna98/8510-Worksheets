The Indianapolis journal October 16, 1899
Will Join the Queen's Army.
URBANA, O., Oct. 14 - Buffalo Bill's Wild West show closed the season here today. 
A feature of the show are fourteen English soldiers, lancers In the Queen's army. After the performance to-night they left by special train for New York. Thence they go to Gibraltar, where they board an English transport steamer for South Africa to join the Queen's army in the Transvaal war.
