Indian Chiefs Daughter Dead. 
Special to the Indianapolis Journal. FORT WAYNE. Ind., Aug. 5. - The twelve-year-old daughter of Chief Strike Plenty, of the Buffalo Bill show, died at 1 at St. Joseph's Hospital, the result of the injuries sustained by her falling from her berth on the train coming from Muncie. She had been i11. The father is a Sioux chief from Dakota.
