Daily Wabash Express August 12, 1886 pg. 1
Buffalo Bill's Prospective Wealth. New York, N Y., August 11.-Wm. F. Cody, better known as "Buffalo Bill," received word to-day from his lawyer at Cleveland, that he is likely to win his suit for the property of Euclid Avenue, Cleveland. The land is valued at $1,500,000, and was illegally transferred by a grand uncle of Mr. Cody.
