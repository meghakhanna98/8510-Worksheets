Daily Tribune Dec. 24 1902 
Buffalo Bill Entertains.
LONDON, Dec. 24-Col. Cody "Buffalo Bill" entertained members of the British and American press and others at Olympian hall last night. There were in all 100 guests. H. Clay [Illegible], United States consul general, responded to the toast "President Roosevelt," and William J. Bull, M. P., replied to the toast "King Edward." The remarks of both speakers were received with enthusiasm.
