The Plymouth Tribune Sept. 5 1907

A Circus Trust.
A circus trust, controlling all the various "greatest shows on earth," will be formed within a month with
Ringling Brothers in control, according to a report current In New York City. It is understood that the Ringling's hold an option on the great Barnum & Bailey circus, which carried with it a contract controlling Buffalo Bill's Wild West shows. The Ring lings already own the Sells Brothers and the Adam Forepaugh shows.
