New Albany Daily Ledger Oct. 5 1898
Col. Cody, "Buffalo Bill," will be in New Albany with his great Wild West Show on the 10th. He laid off couple of days at Kansas City for needed rest, but has again joined the company. Col. Cody is one of the most remarkable men in the country. As an Indian scoot, and a leader in the wars against the Indians west of the Mississippi, be won undying fame Many of the thrilling scenes in his life are illustrated in his Wild West Show that exhibits at New Albany on the 10th inst.
